We found each other working openly.

Our co-operative is designed to help others reap the benefits of open learning, open innovation and open communities.

Some of what we do

the Thinkathon

We use this one-day session to help clients wrestle with their biggest problems, showing where they can get started, and how the open approaches we have adopted and developed can help solve them.

System Mapping

Wondering how this department and that team should collaborate? The We Are Open Co-op can help you find correlation of seemingly disparate pieces and can help you visualize your organizations projects or programs.

Leading Openly

We are open, lifelong learners who use open practices to make the world a better place. We're great at modeling open behaviors and bending technology to the will of open. We can teach you and your peers how to be open and agile leaders.

Community Engagement

Understanding the motivations and desires of people in your community and creating equitable and safe spaces is step one in community engagement. We can help you use open practices and the World Wide Web to interact with your networks to build strong learning communities.
